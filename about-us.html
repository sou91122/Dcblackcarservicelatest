About Black Car Rides

The Management team at Black Car Rides has been in the ground transportation industry well over 10 years. Our most preponderant (Preeminent) objective is to make ground transportation service easier and more accessible for each and everyone one, especially our frequent travelers and corporate customers.

We aim to understand our customer’s needs and wants. Understanding time is of the essence we pride our self on providing a professional and safe service to our customers.

Our philosophy is very simple, we want to wow our customers with the quality of service we provide. Our drivers are honest and hard-working individuals who understand our customers comes first at all times. So we never rest; as long as there are airplanes flying around the globe, corporate meetings to attend, frequent travelers or Family and friends attending concerts and sporting events, our drivers will always be a call away.

We value quality and work with integrity, we will always honor the prices we offer to our customers. We believe the greatest reward we can get as a company is loyalty from our customer’s base on the repeat business we get and the referrals we receive. Every customer is important to Black Car Rides.
